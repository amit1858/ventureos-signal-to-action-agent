## What this product is

An **AI-native Enterprise Revenue Operating System** that continuously turns
fragmented customer signals into governed, explainable, human-approved revenue
actions — then executes, measures, and learns in a closed loop.

```
Signals → Reasoning → Prioritization → Governed Decision → Human Approval
        → Revenue Execution → Business Outcomes → Decision Ledger → Learning
```

Not an assistant. Not a copilot. Not a CRM. Not a dashboard.

## The 30-second pitch

Enterprise revenue teams drown in signals that never say what to *do*.
Dashboards describe, copilots hallucinate, recommendation engines stop at a
list. Signal-to-Action Agent is the operating system that owns the whole
governed loop: a deterministic decision engine ranks the portfolio with cited
evidence; an advisory, provider-abstracted (NVIDIA-ready) reasoning layer
explains and drafts; a hard human gate precedes every action; a Revenue
Execution Center orchestrates approved actions to a business outcome; and an
immutable Decision Ledger records everything. Shipped and live today.
