# Roadmap — Signal-to-Action Agent

> Public roadmap aligned to the **currently deployed** product state. Labels are explicit: **Implemented**, **Next / In Review**, **Planned Hackathon Implementation**, and **Future**. Items are only listed as Implemented once confirmed in the deployed production build.

The product direction remains governed:

> **AI helps explain and recommend. AI does not determine ranking, bypass governance, or execute CRM actions without human approval.**

---

## ✅ Implemented (deployed today)

Live at **https://ventureos-signal-to-action-agent.vercel.app** with backend on Render.

- Executive Command Center
- Executive Daily Briefing
- Executive Change Brief
- Portfolio Pulse
- Revenue Execution Center
- Decision Ledger
- Governance
- Adaptive Experience Modes
- HubSpot integration

### Implemented capability map

| Capability | What it delivers |
|---|---|
| Executive Command Center | AI Chief of Staff narrative framing, Executive Attention Required, and portfolio-level focus |
| Executive Daily Briefing | Daily AI Chief of Staff narrative of what changed and what needs action |
| Executive Change Brief | Change narrative for risk increases, opportunity moves, and queue movement |
| Portfolio Pulse | Risk and opportunity movement across the full book of accounts |
| Revenue Execution Center | Approved action lifecycle from prepared to outcome captured |
| Decision Ledger | Auditable decision and outcome trail |
| Governance | Hard human approval gate, evidence visibility, caveat-first trust model |
| Adaptive Experience Modes | Executive / Seller / Operations lenses on the same governed runtime |
| HubSpot integration | Test CRM sync and approval-gated writeback pathway |
